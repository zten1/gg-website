Frequently asked questions

To join our Minecraft server you have to join our Discord server first. Once you're there a staff member will help you to get on the server!

Can anyone join?

Anyone can join our Discord or Minecraft server. Only requirement is that you have to be atleast 18 years old.

Is it free to join?

What plugins do you have on your Minecraft server?

Our goal is for you to have a vanilla experience while playing on the Minecraft server. But we do run some quality of life datapacks from vanillatweaks such as; Anti Enderman Grief, Armor Statues, Double Shulker Shells and Crafting Tweaks
